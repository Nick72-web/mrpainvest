About Us

What makes us special?

Like you and I, We love beautiful affordable things. Mr PA invest is a mini-project created to offer services within campus area.The services include selling Mabuyu and good looking designer Swimming costumes of both ladies and gents at varying prices depending on one's priorities and the pocket. It’s a negotiable deal.

Mr PA was founded on 2022 and launched in 2023 for campus students who would like to order goods and services online , this will assist save on transport cost as we do free delivery within the institution

We offer a variety of Mabuyu flavours based on your preferred color and taste as some contain chillie spice. The swimming costumes are available at comrade prices. Beside the main agenda we also have fun every weekend through adventure by exploring the beautiful nature we have around Kenya. We do adventurous activities hosted by a company known as Kenda Safaris that provides pocket friendly deals and captivating memorables tours.
